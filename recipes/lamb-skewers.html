Rey's Infamous Lamb Skewers

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1kg Lamb
  • 1 Red onion
  • Wooden skewers
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Cumin
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Dice Lamb into 2cm by 2cm pieces and place into bowl
  2. Slice and crush Red onion and mix with diced lamb, allowing to marinate for 30 mintues
  3. Skewer lamb pieces
  4. Over a blazing hot bbq, cook skewers for 10 mintues, rotating every minute
  5. Sprinkle 1/1/1 mixure of Chilli flakes, cumin and salt as desired for seasoning
  6. Enjoy!
