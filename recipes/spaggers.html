WHO DOESN'T LIKE SPAGGERS - THE GUIDE

Instructions

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups All purpose Flour
  • 3 Large Egg
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Tablesoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 4 Tomatoes
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Red Onion
  • Chilli Flakes

Steps

  1. Sift All Purpose Flour, salt and oil and slowly stir in eggs 1 at a time until nice and doughy
  2. Kneed ball for 10 years until dough is nice and springy and let rest for 1 hour
  3. As Dough is resting, prepare sauce
  4. Super fine dice garlin and onions
  5. In a Pan, sautee garlic and onions until brown
  6. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer until sauce thicc
  7. Add salt, sugar and chilli flakes to taste
  8. After dough is rested, roll out and slice into 1cm strips
  9. Boil spaggers for 7 minutes or until al dente
  10. Stir spaggers into sauce, plate and enjoy! VOILA
