Daiquiri: The only cocktail you need

Instructions

Ingredients

  • 2 Ounces light rum
  • 1 Ounce lime juice, MUST BE FRESHLY SQUEEZED
  • 1 Onces simple syrup
  • Garnish: lime twist

Steps

  1. In a shaker, add rum, lime and simple syrup
  2. Add heaps of ice to shaker
  3. SHAKE BABY for 30 seconds!
  4. Strain into a chilled coupe glass
  5. Garnish with lime twist
  6. Enjoy :)
